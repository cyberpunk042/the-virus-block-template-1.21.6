## üìà Infection Tiers

The Virus spreads through **five escalating tiers**:

### Tier 1 ‚Äî Mild Corruption

- Adjacent blocks mutate into random variants  
- Single-block flickers / texture glitches  
- Passive mobs stare or follow players  
- Loaded chunk: 1 random block mutates every few seconds  
- Virus Aura: *Poison I* near the block (milk cures)

### Tier 2 ‚Äî Environmental Decay

- Water turns murky  
- Random blocks throughout loaded chunks mutate  
- Small cave-ins during mining  
- Hostile arrows occasionally fall from the sky  
- Passive mobs become slightly aggressive

### Tier 3 ‚Äî Hostile Ecosystem

- Zombies infect passive mobs  
- Terrain patches convert to corrupted biomes  
- TNT meteors fall occasionally  
- Trees decay or become twisted  
- Virus Aura upgraded: poison + hunger

### Tier 4 ‚Äî Reality Warping

- Random gravity collapses  
- Blocks fall sideways or upward  
- Chunks visually ‚Äústutter‚Äù  
- Mobs gain random abilities: speed, crits, explosions, venom  
- Fluids misbehave in corrupted zones

### Tier 5 ‚Äî The Endgame

- Biomes collapse or invert  
- Massive TNT/Arrow barrages  
- Terrain waves rise and fall  
- Entity duplication events  
- Virus Block bossbar locks at 100%‚Äîonce it fills, **Apocalypse Mode** begins.

### üîª Apocalypse Mode (Post-Tier 5)

Once the Tier 5 progress bar finishes, every Virus Block sheds its shells, the bossbar switches to a red health readout, and the corruption enters its terminal phase:

- The Virus Block becomes fully vulnerable; melee hits, arrows, TNT, and Purification option 4 all drain its health.
- Purification option 3 still halves the block‚Äôs max health, shortening the fight without rewinding progress.
- The calm/progress bossbar is hidden; only the red health bar remains while vulnerable.
- When Virus HP reaches zero *or* you finish mining the exposed block, the infection is cleansed and the world begins to recover.
- (Future roadmap: a Singularity-style fail-safe may return later if players ignore Apocalypse Mode for too long, but it is currently disabled.)

## ‚öîÔ∏è Ending the Infection

Apocalypse Mode is the last phase‚Äîthere is no separate boss arena right now. Survive the Tier 5 chaos, keep accelerating the Virus Block‚Äôs progress, and once the bar flips to red health you can finally fight back:

- Every hit against the Virus Block removes health instead of rewinding progress.
- TNT, bed explosions, and projectiles all deal chunk-based damage; larger blasts chew away more HP.
- Purification totems remain powerful finishers‚Äîoption 3 reduces max HP, option 4 burns current HP.
- When the health bar empties, the Virus Block disintegrates and the infection resets to zero sources.

## üèõÔ∏è Player Tools & Interactions

### Accelerate Corruption

Feed the Virus Block powerful items to increase its tier faster.

### Delay Corruption

Build a containment structure around it (configurable effects).

### Temporary Reversion

Craft a **Purification Totem** (expensive, late-game).  
Purifies or cancels one mutation event. Choose between:

- **No Boobytraps** ‚Äì disables trap placement and throttles corrupted worm influx.
- **No Shell** ‚Äì collapses every defensive shell wrapped around the Virus Block.
- **Half HP** ‚Äì halves the Virus Block‚Äôs *maximum* health for the current tier, shortening the remaining timeline without triggering shell collapse.
- **Bleed HP** ‚Äì halves the Virus Block‚Äôs *current* health, rolling its boss bar backward immediately.

### End the Apocalypse

Drain the Virus Block‚Äôs health during Apocalypse Mode (or finish mining the exposed block) to wipe the infection.  
The world remains scarred, but no longer worsens.
